Fabric Guide 🧵

Because the secret to a perfect abaya lies in its fabric. Discover our premium materials and find what suits your lifestyle best.

Crepe (كريب) All Seasons

A classic and practical fabric. It has a beautiful drape and elegant look.

  • Best for: Daily wear & Work
  • Durability: Very High

Nada (ندى) Summer Friendly

Lightweight, breathable and luxurious softness.

  • Best for: Summer & Morning outings
  • Durability: High

Linen (كتان) Summer Essential

Highly breathable fabric with a casual elegant look.

  • Best for: Travel & Casual wear
  • Durability: Medium

Velvet (مخمل) Winter Only

Rich and warm fabric perfect for cold weather.

  • Best for: Evening events
  • Durability: High

Silk (حرير) Special Occasions

Luxury fabric with smooth texture and high shine.

  • Best for: Weddings
  • Durability: Delicate

Jacquard (جاكار) Winter / Events

Structured fabric with beautiful woven patterns.

  • Best for: Formal events
  • Durability: Very High
